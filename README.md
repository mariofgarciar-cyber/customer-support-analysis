# Customer Support Tickets Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 8,469 customer support tickets using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

The objective is to identify operational patterns, customer satisfaction trends, ticket distribution, and business improvement opportunities.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* SQL (SQLite)
* Power BI

---

## Dataset

Customer Support Tickets Dataset

Records analyzed: 8,469

---

## Business Questions

* What is the distribution of tickets by status?
* Which ticket types generate the highest support demand?
* Which support channels receive the most tickets?
* How are tickets distributed by priority?
* Which channels generate the highest customer satisfaction?
* How does customer satisfaction vary by ticket priority?

---

## Key Findings

* A total of 8,469 tickets were analyzed.
* Pending Customer Response was the largest ticket status category.
* Refund Requests and Technical Issues generated the highest support demand.
* Ticket volume was relatively balanced across communication channels.
* Chat achieved the highest customer satisfaction.
* Phone achieved the lowest customer satisfaction.
* Low-priority tickets showed the highest customer satisfaction.
* Critical-priority tickets showed the lowest customer satisfaction.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Reduce pending tickets through automated follow-up workflows.
2. Investigate root causes behind refund requests and technical issues.
3. Replicate successful Chat support practices across all channels.
4. Improve Phone support processes to increase customer satisfaction.
5. Strengthen monitoring of critical-priority tickets.
6. Implement SLA tracking dashboards.
7. Expand self-service documentation and knowledge bases.
